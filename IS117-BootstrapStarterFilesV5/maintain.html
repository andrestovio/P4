You are happy with your weight and are afraid that you would either lose or gain weight. Only you know your own body and in control of it. When consuming food, you should neither eat until you feel full or starve yourself. This is all about a balanced diet.

First, we need to separate the disctint types of food such as proteins, fats, legumes, carbohydrates, fruits, vegetables, and beverages.

The breakfast should contain a grapefruit, poached eggs, and a slice 100% whole wheat toast. Another breakfast combination whole-wheat English muffin with tablespoons peanut butter and a orange.

The lunch should contain grilled chicken breast and large garden salad (3 cups mixed greens with 1 cup cherry tomatoes, 1/4 avocado, topped with 2 tablespoons balsamic vinaigrette). Another lunch combination is a turkey sandwich (6 ounces of turkey breast meat, large tomato slice, green lettuce, 1/4 avocado, and 2 teaspoons honey mustard on two slices of whole wheat bread).

The dinner recommendation can be a cup steamed broccoli, a cup of brown rice, and Halibut (4-ounce portion). Another recommendation is a 5-ounce sirloin steak, a roasted sweet potato, a cup cooked spinach (made with teaspoons of olive oil), a cup green beans.
